Hemal Dey

Geographer - Researcher - GIScientist

Biography

I was born in Narsingdi, a small city on the Dhaka division, Bangladesh. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Geography and Environment from Jagannath University, Dhaka. I hold a Master's degree in Cartography and Geographic Information Systems. My master's studies mainly focused on Geographic Information Science (GIS) and Remote Sensing (RS). My Master's studies was focused on flood hazards mapping using geospatial techniques.

Now, I am working as an Research Assistant Department of Geography at the University of Alabama.

I have a long-running research interest in better using geospatial technologies to save life and property in flood disasters. My current research primarily focuses on flood risk simulation using remotely sensed data.
